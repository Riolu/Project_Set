Shanghai Jiao Tong University The Pre-University Program (Liberal Arts) for International Students, 2015中文版 Chinese Version

2015 Cultivation plans for Pre-University Program (liberal arts)

 

Application Prerequisites

  • 1. Non-Chinese citizen

  • 2. The applicants of mainland China, Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan who have immigrated to other countries must hold foreign visa or nationality certificate for 4 years or above, and in the last 4 years (before May 30, 2015) must actually have lived abroad more than 2 years (more than 9 months of residence abroad per year may be considered as one year, according to the entry and exit seals). Applicants, who were citizens of China, should provide:①Certificate of Naturalization; ②Certificate of original Chinese household deregistration

  • 3. Senior high school (or above) diploma or equivalent should be held. Applicants under 18 years old should submit Guardian’s Guarantee Statement

  • 4. Applicants should have basic knowledge of Chinese language

  •  

  • Contents of the program
    The program is for applicants who will apply undergraduate (liberal arts) program in Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Studying subjects cover Chinese, Math and English. Teaching Language is Chinese.

Students who pass the graduation test of the program can directly apply the majors in the following:

Application Materials

  • 1. Senior high school diploma or equivalent (Graduating students should provide an official statement serving as proof of graduation)

  • 2. Official transcripts of your academic achievement in high school study or equivalent, including courses taken

  • 3. A minimum requirement of HSK 4 (180 or above) to prove your Chinese language proficiency

  • 4. cOne photocopy of passport

  • 5. Application fee CNY 800 (approx. US$ 140, can be paid online through application website, and will not be refunded.)

  • ☆ The first two materials should be notarized by official institution, if they’re in other languages other than Chinese/English.

  •  

  • Application Deadline
    July 15, 2015

 

Studying time
From September 7, 2015 (Monday) to May 13, 2016 (Friday)

 

Application Procedure
Step 1. Apply online
Application website: http://isc.sjtu.edu.cn
Step 2. The paper documents should be sent to Shanghai Jiao Tong University by mail

 

Scholarships
The scholarships will be awarded according to the transcripts applicants offer. The scholarships will cover tuition fee or part of tuition fee. Applicants should not be awarded other scholarships at the same time.

 

Fees

  • 1. Tuition fee: CNY 29,800/academic year

  • 2. Accommodation fee: CNY 35/day/person (triple room)

  • 3. Miscellaneous fee: CNY 3,500/academic year (including materials, student activities, insurance and other expenses)
