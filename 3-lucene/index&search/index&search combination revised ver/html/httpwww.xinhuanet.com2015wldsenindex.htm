China Xinhua News Network Corporation (CNC) is a TV news network run by Xinhua News Agency targeting overseas audiences.

On Jan. 1, 2010, CNC officially kicked off its first owned and operated (O&O) TV station -- CNC Chinese channel, which was broadcast to audiences in parts of Europe and the Asia-Pacific region.

CNC stands ready to cover major worldwide events in a timely, accurate and authoritative manner with a global perspective.
