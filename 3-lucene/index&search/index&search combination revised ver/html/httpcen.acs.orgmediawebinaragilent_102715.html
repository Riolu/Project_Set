Orthogonal Chromatography in Pharmaceutical Drug and Impurity Analysis: Part 1
Tuesday October 27th 2015
First Session: USA 9:00 am EDT, 6:00 am PDT, 13:00 GMT, 14:00 CET
Repeat Session: USA 12:00 pm EST, 9:00 am PST, 17:00 GMT, 18:00 CET
SPEAKER
CJ Venkatramani, Ph.D.,
Senior Scientist,
Analytical Development and QC,
Agilent Technologies, Inc.
MODERATOR
Celia Arnaud,
Senior Editor,
C&EN
OVERVIEW

In this two-part webinar series, we discuss strategies that address the analytical challenges encountered in pharmaceutical and impurity analysis through greater utilization of multidimensional chromatography and supercritical fluid chromatography (SFC). Genentech, a member of the Roche Group, uses these technologies routinely in drug development, and we present examples of real-world use.

KEY LEARNING OBJECTIVES:

- Brief overview of 2D-LC and 2D-LC/SFC

- Applications of 2D-LC and 2D-LC/MS in small molecule pharmaceutical analysis including method developent strategies for co-eluting impurities

- Applications of multidimensional chromatography in simultaneous achiral-chiral analysis
