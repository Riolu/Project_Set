Journal of Shanghai Jiaotong University(English. Edition)

时间：2010年11月01日 09:32 发布人： 浏览次数：[]
　　《上海交通大学学报》（英文版）是由上海交通大学主办的自然科学与工程技术的学术期刊。它独立于其中文版，现暂定为半年刊（刊号ISSN1007-1172；CN31-1716/U）。上海交通大学建校已有100多年，是中国著名的重点高等院校之一。上海交通大学学报编辑委员会由来自该校各个学科的资深教授和专家组成。刊物兼顾学术性和应用性，以工程技术应用为主。本刊旨在反映本校师生和科研人员的最新科研成果，促进校内外及国内外的科技学术交流。本刊学科内容涉及船舶及海洋工程、材料工程、动力机械工程、工程力学、生物工程、机械工程、能源工程、电力工程、电子信息工程、塑性成型工程、计算机科学及自动化等方面。

Journal of Shanghai Jiaotong University
(English. Edition)

　　Journal of Shanghai Jiaotong University (English edition) is an academic publication focusing on natural science and engineering technology in a comprehensive way. It is sponsored by Shanghai Jiaotong University, which was founded early in 1896 and is one of the most famous universities in China. As an independent Journal of its Chinese edition, it is distributed both at home and abroad as a semiannual (ISSN 1007-1172). The Editorial Section of the Journal is subjected to the guidance of the Editorial Board of the Journal of Shanghai Jiaotong University, the members of which are all well –known professors and specialists. The purpose of the Journal is to reflect most up-to-date scientific research achievements in the university and to promote an academic exchange between home and abroad, The selected subjects of the Journal involve Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering, Materials Engineering, power Machinery Engineering, Engineering Mechanics, Biological Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Energy Resources Engineering, Electric power Engineering, Electronic Engineering, plastic Formation Engineering, Computer Science and Automation, etc.
